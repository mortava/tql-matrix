DSCR Short-Term Rental (STR) -- Proof of Rent Requirements
Detailed STR documentation methods by investor
Investor Solutions / Verus DSCR STR

Max LTV P: 70-75% Max LTV Refi: 65-70% 20% Expense Factor DSCR Min 1.0

Method 1: STR Analysis Form or 1007/1025 by Licensed Appraiser
  • Must include source of data used
  • Include comparable STR properties focusing on room count, GLA, location, market appeal
  • Include daily rental rate and occupancy percentage
  • Factor seasonality and vacancy into analysis
  • Must be completed by a licensed appraiser
Method 2: 12-Month Rental History from 3rd Party Rental/Management Service
  • Statement must identify the subject property/unit
  • Rents collected for previous 12 months
  • All vendor management fees listed
  • Qualifying income must be net of all vendor or management fees
Method 3: 12-Month Bank Statements from Borrower
  • Must evidence short-term rental deposits
  • Borrower must provide rental records for subject property to support monthly deposits
Method 4: AirDNA Rentalizer / Property Earning Potential Report
  • Purchase transactions ONLY
  • Gross rents = revenue projection less 20% extraordinary expense factor
  • Forecast period: 12 months, dated within 90 days of Note date
  • Maximum occupancy: 2 individuals per bedroom
  • Must have 3 comparable properties (size, room count, amenities, availability, occupancy)
  • Market Score or Sub-Market Score must be >= 60

Expense Factor: 20% minimum. If actual expenses > 20%, use actual expenses. Formula: (Gross Rents * 0.80) / PITIA = DSCR. Income based on 12-month average for seasonality.

LoanStream Core DSCR STR

Experienced Investors Only 20% Mgmt Fee Reduction DSCR 1.0 min (1.20 if >80%)

Rent Documentation
  • Rents documented with 1007/1025 supported by 12 months history of payments OR AirDNA/Overview Report
  • Purchases: 12+ months STR rental history in last 3 years required
  • If < 12 months STR rental history: additional 5% LTV reduction
  • 20% management fee reduction applied
  • Vacant properties allowed
  • Rural NOT allowed on STR
  • DSCR Fusion / Sub-1 / FN / No-Ratio: ineligible for STR

Property types: 1 Unit SFR, 2-4 Unit, PUD, Condo

AHL INVEST STAR DSCR STR

Max LTV P: 75% R&T 70% / C/O 70% DSCR Min 1.00

Purchase & Refinance
  • 100% of income documented by 12-month history of deposits from eligible sources (Airbnb, VRBO)
Purchase Only
  • 75% of income documented by 1007 or property management report
  • 700 FICO required for 75% max LTV purchase
  • 680 FICO minimum for program entry
  • 0x30x12 mortgage rating required
  • Max $1,500,000 loan amount
  • Not permitted on 5-10 unit properties
  • FICO 680-719 & LTV>70% OR 660-679 & LTV>65%: Max $750K loan / $100K cash out
Deephaven DSCR STR

720 Min FICO 1.15x Min DSCR 5% LTV Reduction from Grid 75% Max LTV

Requirements
  • AirDNA/PropertyGuard report required on ALL STR transactions
  • CDA required
  • Purchase: AirDNA report, min 60% Occupancy Rate. Income = 80% of annual revenue / 12
  • Refinance: 12 months documented STR rental history
  • Must document 12 months short-term rental operator experience
  • No first-time investors
  • No 2+ Unit / Rural / Unique Properties
